Direct Admissions at MADE EASY Centre

Take a printout of submitted registration form & submit it along with the fee at concerned MADE EASY office. Fee can be paid through Cash/DD/Debit or Credit Card at the respective centre. 

Mandatory Documents:

Registration acknowledgement Form

MADE EASY ID-Card
(if you are Ex-MADE EASY student).

Attested copy of Identity Proof (such as Passport/ Voter ID/ Driving License/ PAN Card etc.)

Qualifying Documents in
case of Super Talent & Rank Improvement Batches.

Note:

  • Cheque payment is not accepted.
  • Candidates who are willing to pay through DD are advised to register online before proceeding for DD preparation or call us on 9021300500 to get DD details.
  • Write your Full Name, Branch of Engineering and telephone number on the backside of the Demand Draft.

Admissions through Demand Draft (By Post) 

The candidates, unable to reach the office in person, can follow the following procedure for admission in the Classroom Courses:

Take a printout of submitted registration form and send it along with the Demand Draft to the respective centre.

Mandatory Documents to send along with Registration acknowledgement & DD:

1 Passport size
color photograph.

MADE EASY ID-Card
(if you are Ex-MADE EASY student).

Attested copy of Identity Proof (such as Passport/ Voter ID/ Driving License/ PAN Card etc.)

Qualifying Documents in
case of Super Talent & Rank Improvement Batches.

Note:

  • Candidates are advised to register online before proceeding for DD preparation or call us on 9021300500 to get DD details.
  • Admission in the registered batch is subject to availability of seats at the time we receive your DD at the respective office.
  • Write your Full Name, Branch of Engineering and contact number on the backside of the Demand Draft.

Online Admissions at MADE EASY Centre

For our Classroom Programs, you may also apply through the Online Admissions Procedure.

Complete the final registration by making the payment through any one of the following modes:

Modes of Payment:

Debit Card

Credit Card

Net Banking
Passport/ Voter ID/ Driving License/ PAN Card etc.)

Student needs to pay the full fees at the time of admission only or in installments after reading the Terms & Conditions of installment facility.

After successful completion of registration, receipt will be generated.
Click on the Print button or Save button given at the end.

Note:

  • For every batch, there are a limited number of seats available through online admissions.
  • Once the batch and centre is allotted, request for transfer of batch or centre will not be permitted.
  • Classroom entry & issue of material will be only after you procure your MADE EASY ID Card.
  • Payment confirmation & ID Card will be issued after 48 hrs. of registration.

Save the receipt and bring a hard copy of this receipt, two passport size coloured photographs and original & photocopy of identity proof for verifying your candidature & receiving MADE EASY ID Card at:

  • Corporate Office: 44 - A/1, Kalu Sarai, Near Hauz Khas Metro Station, New Delhi-110016 (for all Delhi & NCR Study Centres).
  • For other Centres, kindly visit the admission desk of respective cities i.e.
