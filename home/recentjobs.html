MADE EASY Job Box

‘Job box’, sounds interesting? Yes, it is, especially if you are a Government job aspirant who has recently completed graduation in one of the engineering streams (or even in your final year). As the name suggests, Job Box is a FREE Job Alert that offers detailed information regarding recent Engineering jobs. It primarily includes Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Computer Science and Chemical Engineering Government jobs. MADE EASY experts started this FREE Job Alert to offer the correct and verified information to the Government aspirants.

Key features:

  • Find all the solutions in one place.
  • Get the latest updates about Government Engineering jobs for engineers from different streams.
  • Get the fastest PSU jobs notifications updates.
  • Receive ‘how to apply’ guidance for a smooth application process in the PSU jobs through GATE.
  • Hassle-free experience that saves a lot of time in finding Government Jobs updates.
  • Increased focus to prepare for the competitive exams, instead of finding application form availability and exam dates.

How will it help engineers?

We often hear that there are fewer opportunities for engineers in India. However, the reality is far different from the discussions going on around us. PSU Jobs through GATE and other exams such as ESE, SSC-JE, IRMSE, etc. offer a wide range of opportunities in the form of Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Computer Science and Chemical Engineering Government Jobs. You will find real information about recruitment updates and results of prestigious roles such as Junior Engineers, Assistant Engineers, and several other roles for engineers at top PSUs.

Some of these PSUs include Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL), Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL), Airports Authority of India (AAI), Hindustan Copper Limited (HCL), National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC), and Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL), Mangalore Refinery and Petrochemicals Limited (MRPL), and many more. Therefore, if you are thinking about where to find information about recent engineering jobs in the Government sector, MADE EASY Job Box is the best solution for you.

So, bookmark this page and keep an eye out for the latest PSU Jobs notifications.
