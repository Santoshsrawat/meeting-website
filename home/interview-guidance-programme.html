Interview Guidance Programme

  • Interview Guidance Programme for ESE
  • Interview Guidance Programme for PSUs
 
 

About Course

UPSC interview panel consists of experts of various fields having a wide experience of around 20-25 years in the technical section. You may think you are technically strong as you have cleared Mains, but whatever you have learned till now is bookish knowledge, but they will grill you on practical application. They don’t want engineer boy, rather they are searching for Engineer Men to fit into the system of practical approach. So to connect your learned book concepts with the practical application you need guidance from experts who can train you well. Needles to say MADE EASY has the unmatchable expertise to mentor every Mains qualified candidate to handle this sensitive area.

Remember, this is not the conventional paper where if one of your answers goes wrong then that can be covered in other questions; in an interview every word counts, one unsuitable word will take your interview in another direction and then recovery in the entire interview time is not possible. Henceforth, if you don’t give suitable answers your overall rank goes down irrespective of your Mains performance. This point makes it a must need to have proper interview handling guidance and that too from the best mentors.

MADE EASY has analyzed UPSC pattern which definitely shows the track- where they emphasize and what type of questions they can ask and what answers they expect. To handle them well we give an exclusive Interview Guidance course for ESE Mains qualified candidates where the team grooms you well so that the question put up to you is not the first time.

Interview Guidance Course not only gives you strength in Current Affairs, HR and technical areas but also focuses on improving your overall personality for the interview; the point when you greet them till you say thank you all eyes are on you and you will be judged on your every action. You obviously cannot slouch or lean back in front of interviewer: the ideal way to sit, the best dress for the day, the best way to greet the panel, the best way to introduce yourself such areas are also equally taken care of in the Interview Guidance Program.

MADE EASY organizes mock interviews simulating UPSC environment with renowned and experienced panel members who are retired civil servants, professors and personality grooming experts with wide experience. You can give mock interviews, implement the learned tips, get rid of the fear of interview and make your UPSC interview the best interview ever.

Features

  • Personality Development & HR Sessions
  • Tips to face interview
  • Discussion on
    • DAF & personal profile
    • Previous interview questions
    • Hobbies, Interests, Degree Project, Extra & Co-curricular Activities etc.
  • Technical sessions
  • General Awareness and Current Affairs
  • One to One discussion to deal with personal profile related questions
  • Mock interview with real time UPSC interview environment
  • Video recording of Mock Interviews and feedback analysis

Note: Recorded videos will also be available for the candidates, who are unable to attend these sessions.

About Course

After the preparation phase for GATE, the preparation is still not over until you clear the next hurdle which is GD/GT and personal interviews at various PSUs. On hearing this word Interview many students get nervous with many questions coming in their minds – how to answer? What to say for the gap in my studies? What should be the ideal way to greet the panel? If selected in more than one PSU then which should I join? ..... and many more.

Every candidate must understand the purpose of interview, which is simple they have to select an employee who has technical knowledge but also knows to handle practical situations. You may be strong on your technical part but the practical approach area is the area which is now to be focused. Interviewers may ask simple definitions and they may also ask the application of read concept.

Also, interview is not like the OMR sheet where you are given rough sheets to check your answer; in interview every word counts and you cannot afford to say even a single word wrong as in 15-20 min you will not get chance to recover your mistake . This 15-20 min of conversation decides whether you will be hired or not; so interview has to be taken very seriously. To lessen the burden and to make interview preparation easy, MADE EASY gives Interview Guidance to all written test qualified candidates round the year as and when results of PSUs are declared.

Interview Guidance Course not only gives you strength in HR and technical areas but also focuses on improving your overall personality for the interview; the point when you greet them till you say thank you all eyes are on you and you will be judged on your every action. You obviously cannot slouch or lean back in front of interviewer: the ideal way to sit, the best dress for the day, the best way to greet the panel, the best way to introduce yourself such areas are also equally taken care of in the Interview Guidance Program.

MADE EASY organises mock interviews, consisting of renowned and experienced panel members who are retired civil servants, professors and personality grooming experts with wide experience. You can give mock interviews, implement the learnt tips, get rid of fear of interview and thereby boost your chance of getting selected in the final round.

Documents Required:

Photocopy of MADE EASY I-Card, One Photograph, Photocopy of call letter, Proof of selection & GATE Score card.

Note:

Interview sessions will be conducted only for those candidates who have cleared the written examination of PSUs / State Level Engineering Services / SSC /SSB / IITs etc.
